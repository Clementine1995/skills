# Selection Guide

Use this as a routing guide for `VoltAgent/awesome-design-md`. The repository can change, so fetch live data when exact availability matters.

## Common Matches

### Developer Tools and AI Products

- `vercel`: Minimal, high-contrast, developer-focused, strong for deployment, cloud, docs, and technical SaaS.
- `cursor`: AI coding, editor-like surfaces, dark technical mood, strong for code assistants and agent workflows.
- `warp`: Terminal, CLI, command surfaces, developer productivity, strong for technical power tools.
- `supabase`: Open-source developer platform, database/admin surfaces, friendly technical credibility.
- `sentry`: Monitoring, debugging, incident workflows, dense technical dashboards.
- `hashicorp`: Infrastructure, enterprise developer tools, technical trust.
- `clickhouse`: Data infrastructure, analytics, performance-heavy technical products.
- `x.ai`: AI-native, stark, futuristic, high contrast.
- `claude`: Calm AI assistant surfaces, writing, analysis, soft productivity.
- `together.ai`: AI infrastructure and model platform positioning.

### SaaS, Productivity, and Operational Dashboards

- `linear`: Crisp, fast, elegant workflow tools, issue tracking, roadmaps, internal tools.
- `airtable`: Database-like productivity, flexible records, collaborative business tools.
- `notion`: Documents, knowledge bases, calm productivity, creator workflows.
- `slack`: Collaboration, team communication, approachable enterprise.
- `superhuman`: Premium productivity, speed, email/workflow tools.
- `zapier`: Automation, integrations, approachable workflow building.
- `cal`: Scheduling, calendar workflows, clean utility.
- `figma`: Creative collaboration, design tooling, multiplayer product surfaces.
- `framer`: Visual website building, creator tools, polished previews.

### Finance, Commerce, and Trust-Heavy Products

- `stripe`: Payments, fintech, commercial SaaS, trust, clean data and conversion.
- `wise`: Consumer finance, money movement, transparent and friendly trust.
- `coinbase`: Crypto finance, portfolio and exchange products.
- `binance`: Crypto trading, high-density financial surfaces.
- `shopify`: Commerce, merchant tools, storefront/admin workflows.
- `ibm`: Enterprise, regulated systems, serious B2B trust.

### Consumer, Marketplace, and Lifestyle

- `airbnb`: Marketplace, travel, hospitality, warm consumer trust.
- `apple`: Premium consumer product, hardware/software launch pages, refined restraint.
- `spotify`: Music, entertainment, dark energetic consumer experiences.
- `uber`: Mobility, logistics, urban utility, clean consumer operations.
- `starbucks`: Food/beverage, loyalty, warm brand-led retail.
- `webflow`: Website builder, no-code, creator-facing marketing and product pages.

### Editorial and Media

- `wired`: Technology journalism, bold editorial layouts, high personality.
- `theverge`: Tech media, colorful editorial energy, content-heavy experiences.
- `notion`: Long-form documentation and lightweight knowledge products.
- `apple`: Premium editorial product storytelling.

### Luxury, Automotive, and High-Drama Product Marketing

- `tesla`: Futuristic automotive/energy, stark launches, product drama.
- `bmw`: Premium automotive, polished engineering.
- `bmw-m`: Performance automotive, sharper and sportier than BMW.
- `ferrari`: High-emotion luxury performance.
- `bugatti`: Ultra-luxury, exclusivity, dramatic product presentation.
- `spacex`: Aerospace, mission-driven, cinematic technical ambition.

### Playful, Expressive, or Niche References

- `dell-1996`: Retro computing, nostalgic product pages, intentionally old-web aesthetics.
- `clay`: People/data enrichment, modern B2B sales/productivity tone.
- `composio`: AI agent/tool integration surfaces.
- `voltagent`: Agent framework and AI developer tooling context.

## Fast Decision Heuristics

- Dense admin surface: start with Linear, Stripe, Airtable, Sentry, or Supabase.
- Developer homepage: start with Vercel, Supabase, Cursor, Warp, or HashiCorp.
- AI assistant app: start with Claude, Cursor, x.ai, Vercel, or Superhuman.
- Fintech dashboard: start with Stripe, Wise, Coinbase, or IBM.
- Commerce tool: start with Shopify, Stripe, Airtable, or Zapier.
- Consumer launch page: start with Apple, Airbnb, Spotify, Uber, or Tesla.
- Editorial/content product: start with WIRED, The Verge, Notion, or Apple.
- Luxury launch: start with Apple, Tesla, BMW M, Ferrari, Bugatti, or SpaceX.

## Mixing Guidance

- Linear + Vercel: restrained developer/productivity tools with crisp execution.
- Stripe + Linear: business SaaS with trustworthy data-heavy flows.
- Claude + Superhuman: calm AI productivity with premium speed.
- Cursor + Warp: technical AI/devtool interfaces with dark immersive focus.
- Apple + Vercel: polished product launch with developer credibility.
- Airbnb + Shopify: warm consumer marketplace or commerce experiences.

Use mixing sparingly. Pick one primary style and borrow one secondary trait, such as copy tone, density, motion, or color restraint.
